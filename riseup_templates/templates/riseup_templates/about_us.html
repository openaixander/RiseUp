About RiseUp

Rise Up is a self-discipline and recovery platform built to help individuals overcome pornography addiction through daily accountability, encouragement, and community support.

Why We Built Rise Up

We understand the struggle of addiction—especially when it's silent and shame-filled. That's why we built Rise Up: to offer a safe space to fight back daily, track progress, and never feel alone.

Many of us have experienced firsthand how addictive habits can silently take control of our lives. We've felt the isolation, the shame, and the frustration of trying to break free on our own. Rise Up was born from this shared experience—a tool we wished we had during our own journeys to recovery.

Our mission is simple: to provide practical tools for daily accountability combined with the strength of community support, all while respecting your privacy and personal journey.

What the App Does

Rise Up provides practical, effective tools designed to support your journey to freedom:

Daily Check-ins

Simple, consistent accountability to keep you on track and build momentum.

Personal Challenge Tracking

Set custom goals and watch your progress grow day by day.

Achievement System

Earn badges and celebrate milestones to stay motivated on your journey.

Community Support

Connect anonymously with others who understand your struggle.

Relapse Logs & Encouragement

Learn from setbacks and receive support to start again stronger.

Who This Is For

Whether you're on Day 0 or Day 100, Rise Up is for anyone who wants to build self-control and live a life free from unhealthy habits.

Beginners

Just starting your journey and looking for structure and support to build momentum.

On The Path

Making progress but seeking additional accountability and community to stay consistent.

Recovery Veterans

Looking to maintain long-term freedom and perhaps guide others on their journey.

Privacy & Respect Promise

We value your privacy. Your data is safe, your identity is protected, and your healing journey is respected. No judgment. No pressure. Just progress.

Data Protection

Your personal information and usage data are encrypted and securely stored. We never sell your data to third parties.

Anonymous Participation

Choose how much or how little you want to share. Your identity is yours to reveal or keep private.

Judgment-Free Zone

Every journey has ups and downs. Our platform is designed to encourage, not shame, at every step.

Our Team

Rise Up was developed by a passionate team of believers in discipline, purpose, and recovery. We are not therapists, just real people building tools that we needed ourselves.
